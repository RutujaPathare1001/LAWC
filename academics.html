Faculty of Arts

The Faculty of Arts offers a strong foundation in humanities and social sciences, fostering critical thinking, communication skills, cultural awareness, and social responsibility. The programmes prepare students for higher education, competitive examinations, research, and diverse career opportunities.

  • English
  • Marathi
  • Hindi
  • History
  • Economics
  • Political Science
  • Sociology
  • Physical Education

Faculty of Commerce

The Faculty of Commerce equips students with knowledge of business, finance, accounting, and economics. The curriculum emphasizes practical learning, entrepreneurship, financial management, and professional development to meet the demands of the modern business world.

  • Accountancy
  • Business Studies
  • Banking & Finance
  • Business Economics

Faculty of Science

The Faculty of Science provides quality education through theoretical knowledge, practical laboratory experience, and scientific research. The faculty encourages innovation, analytical thinking, and problem-solving skills, preparing students for careers in science, technology, research, and higher education.

  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Botany
  • Zoology
  • Mathematics
  • Computer Science
